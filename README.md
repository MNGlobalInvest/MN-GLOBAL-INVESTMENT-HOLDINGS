# MN GLOBAL INVESTMENT HOLDINGS, LLC
**Autonomous Bitcoin Accumulation Protocol**

## Overview

MN Global Investment Holdings, LLC is a protocol-driven enterprise dedicated to acquiring and securely holding Bitcoin. All operations, revenue streams, and technology systems are designed to maximize Bitcoin accumulation through autonomous, rules-based processes.

This repository serves as the authoritative reference for the company’s operational framework, financial systems, technology infrastructure, and protocol-driven processes, providing transparency, auditability, and scalability.


## Mission & Vision

**Mission:**  
Acquire and securely hold Bitcoin using protocol-driven operations, ensuring all resources are dedicated to maximizing Bitcoin accumulation.

**Vision:**  
Operate as an autonomous, self-scaling enterprise where every system, asset, and decision reinforces Bitcoin accumulation.


## Core Principles

1. **Single-Minded Purpose** – All revenue, assets, and operations exist solely to acquire Bitcoin.  
2. **100% Bitcoin Balance Sheet** – Every dollar, asset, or profit is converted into Bitcoin.  
3. **Protocol-Based Operations** – Rules-based, automated processes manage all operations with minimal human intervention.  
4. **Self-Scaling & Reinforcing** – Revenue streams, automation, and products maximize Bitcoin inflow.  
5. **Security & Resilience** – Multi-signature wallets, cold storage, and automated monitoring safeguard all assets.
